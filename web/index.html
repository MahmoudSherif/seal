How it works.

Everything happens in your browser. Your files never upload to a server. Your password is used to derive an encryption key on this page and is then thrown away. There is no account, no log, no analytics, no third party.

Cipher

AES-256-GCM authenticated encryption. Any single-bit tampering fails decryption with a clear error.

Key derivation

scrypt(N=2¹⁷, r=8, p=1) — about a second per attempt to make brute-forcing expensive.

Streaming chunks

64 KiB chunks, individually authenticated, with a flag bound into the AEAD nonce that catches truncation and extension.

Folder support

Folders are packed as tar.gz in the browser, encrypted, and delivered as a single .seal file.

Same format, three tools

Files made here open with the command-line and desktop tools — and vice versa.

Open source

MIT licensed. Audit the source, run it offline, host your own copy. The page itself is the entire app.
